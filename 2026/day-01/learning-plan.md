# 🚀 My DevOps Journey

## 📖 My Understanding of DevOps

DevOps is more than just a set of tools—it's a culture that bridges the gap between development and operations teams. It emphasizes collaboration, automation, Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment (CI/CD), and efficient software delivery while ensuring reliability, scalability, and security.

What excites me the most about DevOps is the opportunity to work with technologies like **Linux, Cloud Computing, Networking, Automation, Docker, Kubernetes, and Infrastructure as Code**, while solving real-world engineering challenges.

I recently completed my **Master of Computer Applications (MCA) in 2026**. Throughout my academic journey, I developed a strong interest in **cloud computing, Linux, servers, infrastructure, and automation**, which inspired me to pursue a career in DevOps.
